About me

Disclaimer: not related to the homonymous writer

Hi! I'm a 23 year old computer engineer that loves everything related with them. I'm a native of the Basque Country, exactly from the town of Lekeitio, in Biscay. Thus, I am a native speaker of both Basque and Spanish. I can also speak English fluently on an advanced level.

I'm particularly interested in its interaction with society, in the forms of artificial intelligence, computational science, data mining, and so on. I also like the classics, though: programming, networking, software engineering, computer architecture, Operative Systems and Information security.

I am a self-educated person: I like studying by myself (it's hard but highly rewarding), looking at some technologies or knowledge that have either not seen on class or being key to pursue my career.

I don't like the "workaholic" philosophy but I get work done when it needs to be done, responsible but with no high pressure. Being stressed worsens the quality of the work.

If you want to navigate through the page, the Résumé and Contact pages are self-explanatory. The Skills page shows the technologies that I'm mostly knowledgeable about, and the Coding page shows some work I've done for college or in my spare time.

I also have a blog (static, with no comments), where I jot down some of my thoughs in a wide array of areas.
